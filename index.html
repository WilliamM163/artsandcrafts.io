Welcome to Awhitu Orchard!

We are a family-owned apple orchard dedicated to providing a fun and educational experience for apple pickers of all ages.

Enjoy a day in the fresh air, surrounded by our beautiful orchard and pick your own delicious apples.

Apple Varieties

We offer a wide variety of apples, each with its unique flavor and ripening time.

  • Granny Smith - Known for its bright green skin and tart, tangy flavor. Perfect for eating fresh or using in pies and salads.
  • Gala - This sweet and juicy apple has a beautiful red and yellow skin. Great for snacking and salads.
  • Honeycrisp - This popular variety boasts a super crisp texture and a sweet, slightly tart flavor. Excellent for eating fresh.
