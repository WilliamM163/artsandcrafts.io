About Me

My name is William Massey, and I'm a passionate Computer Science student at the University of Auckland. While I'm diving deep into the world of algorithms and data structures, I'm also fueling my fascination with building things by learning Full Stack Development on the side. I'm particularly interested in HTML, CSS, JS, and Flutter mobile App development.

Beyond academics, I recently developed a Wellbeing App focused on measuring student wellbeing. This project helped me explore the intersection of technology and mental health, and it was incredibly rewarding to see it in use, and to see its impact on the people around me.

When I'm not coding, I enjoy playing hockey, going to the gym, and watching a good TV series on Netflix. I believe a well-rounded life fuels creativity and problem-solving, which are essential in the tech world!
